CONNECT TO YOUR SMARTPHONE

To use a salapsapwatch, you need a smartphone. On your smartphone, you can install the app that comes with the salapsapwatch, such as Galaxy Wearable, Garmin Connect, or Watch by Apple. If you open the accompanying app on your smartphone and turn on Bluetooth, you can synchronize the watch with your smartphone. Now, your devices are connected and you'll receive notifications from your smartphone on the screen of your salapsapwatch.

MEASURING DAILY ACTIVITIES & EXCERCISE

If the salapsapwatch has a built-in GPS and/or heart rate sensor, the watch tracks your daily activities. This includes counting the number of steps, stairs, calories burned, and sleep pattern. Thanks to the GPS, a watch also measures your route while you exercise. Doesn't the smartwatch have a built-in GPS? It's often possible to use the GPS signal from your smartphone. In this case, you do have to take your smartphone with you when you go for a run or bike ride.
